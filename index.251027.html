Our services

Build Community: Volunteer Service

We have been coaching interns, fresh grads and even mid-career professionals to understand the data science/machine learning field. We run seminars and training sessions to help them to get the missing skills that they need to be successful. In some cases, we have some projects from our sponsors, we will coach them on finishing these projects in professional way so that they will get credible experiences to support their career aspiration.

Build Team: Referral Service

Because we work with many talents ranging from fresh grads, scientists at all level, including some renowned data scientists. Therefore, we understand the field well enough so that we can help to find the talents your organization needed to advance your organization aspiration in big data, advanced analytics and data science.

Build Process: Consulting Service

From the successful data science innovation we did in the past, we have a process to produce predictable outcomes. Many data science initiatives failed because many of them run them as research projects. However, we are taking pragmatic approach toward data science to strike a balance between research and desirable outcome to guarantee the team will deliver.
